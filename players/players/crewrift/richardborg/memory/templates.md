Canonical meeting templates

- I saw {player} and {other} together.
- I saw {player} with {victim} shortly before {victim} died.
- I saw {player} near {victim}'s body.
- I saw {player} at a vent.
- I saw {player} at a task.
- I directly confirmed {player} as an imposter from a kill or vent transition.

Use these as evidence statements, not as guaranteed conclusions unless the line
says the player was directly confirmed.
