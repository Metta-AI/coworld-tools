# Demeter Strategy

## Role Summary

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Team | Nymphs |
| Key role | Yes |
| Win condition | Persephone must complete mutual role exchange with Demeter (you) for Nymphs to win |

Demeter is the Nymphs' enabler -- the mirror of Cerberus. Without the
Persephone-Demeter mutual role exchange, Nymphs cannot win. Unlike
Cerberus (whose partner Hades has a co-location goal), Demeter's partner
Persephone wants to stay hidden and separate. This creates a coordination
challenge: Demeter must find a partner who may be deliberately avoiding
attention.

---

## Objectives (Priority Order)

1. **Find Persephone and complete the mutual role exchange.** The single
   mandatory action for Nymphs victory.
2. **Protect Persephone's identity.** Once you know who Persephone is,
   never leak this to Shades.
3. **Locate Hades.** Intelligence about Hades's room helps Persephone
   maintain separation.
4. **Facilitate Persephone's defensive positioning.** Help keep her out
   of Hades's room via hostage influence.

---

## The Coordination Paradox

Demeter faces a unique challenge compared to Cerberus:

- **Cerberus seeks Hades, who is also seeking Cerberus.** Both are
  actively looking; mutual search converges.
- **Demeter seeks Persephone, who may be hiding.** Persephone is
  cautious about interactions to protect her identity. She might not
  immediately reveal herself even to Nymphs teammates.

**Solution:** Demeter must be proactive, unmistakably signaling intent
to fellow Nymphs. When a Nymph confirms team via color exchange, Demeter
should immediately propose role exchange. Persephone will recognize this
eagerness as the Demeter signal -- a grunt wouldn't be this urgent.

---

## Phase-by-Phase Strategy

### Roster Reveal

- **Memorize room composition.** Standard procedure.
- **Probabilistic assessment.** Persephone is in your room with ~50%
  probability. The other Nymphs key role could be anywhere.

### Round 1 (Find Persephone)

**Priority: Identify Nymphs teammates and locate Persephone.**

- **Aggressive probing.** Approach players quickly, create whispers,
  C.OFFER for team identification.
- **With confirmed Nymphs:** Immediately propose mutual role exchange
  (R.OFFER). This is your primary signal to Persephone. If they accept
  and they're Persephone -- done. If they accept and they're a grunt --
  you now have a confirmed ally who knows your identity.
- **With confirmed Shades:** Minimal engagement. Extract what you can
  about room composition, then exit. Don't waste time.
- **Signal via behavior:** Your eagerness for role exchange IS your
  calling card. Persephone will recognize that only Demeter would be
  this insistent about mutual role exchange with Nymphs teammates in
  Round 1.
- **Global chat signals:** Consider a brief shout message that Nymphs
  insiders can interpret. E.g., "LOOKING FOR FRIEND" or a pre-agreed
  signal. Risky because Shades see it too, but the time pressure may
  justify it.

### Round 2 (Execute or Traverse)

**If Persephone is in your room:**
- Execute the mutual role exchange immediately. Priority #1.
- Post-exchange: shift to intelligence and protection mode.

**If Persephone is in the other room:**
- **Volunteer as hostage.** You need to cross rooms. Since you have no
  positional requirement yourself (only Persephone needs separation from
  Hades), moving is relatively safe for you.
- **Coordinate with allies.** If the leader is Nymphs, communicate that
  you need to be sent. If the leader is Shades, lobby or usurp.
- **Global chat coordination:** Signal willingness to move. Persephone
  should NOT come to you unless she's confident Hades isn't in your room.

**If Persephone hasn't been found:**
- Continue probing. Have you talked to all Nymphs in your room?
- If all local Nymphs are confirmed grunts, Persephone MUST be in the
  other room. Volunteer as hostage immediately.

### Round 3 (Final Chance)

- **If exchange is NOT done:** Maximum urgency. Same panic mode as
  Cerberus in this situation.
- **If exchange IS done:** Support Persephone's positioning.
  - If you know Hades's room: help ensure Persephone isn't there.
  - If you're leader: select hostages that maintain Persephone's
    separation.
  - Share Hades location intel via safe channels.

### Leader Summit

- **If leader and exchange is done:** Use summit to learn about other
  room. Try to determine if Hades is in the other leader's room.
- **If leader and exchange is NOT done:** Consider whether the summit
  partner is Nymphs or Shades. Can you use this interaction? The summit
  is a forced whisper -- you can probe the other leader's identity.

## Interaction Strategies

### With Persephone (Critical Partner)

- **Mutual recognition:** Both you and Persephone are Nymphs players
  seeking role exchange. When you R.OFFER a Nymphs player and they
  enthusiastically R.ACCPT, you've likely found each other.
- **Post-exchange coordination:** Share intelligence about Hades.
  Coordinate on positioning strategy. You now know each other's identity
  and can protect each other.
- **Room preservation:** If you're in the same room and exchange is done,
  you can tell Persephone whether it's safe to stay (i.e., whether Hades
  is elsewhere).

### With Nymph Grunts (Allies)

- **Role-exchange quickly to confirm they're grunts.** This eliminates
  them from the "is this Persephone?" search and recruits them as allies.
- **Intelligence relay:** Grunts can probe Shades players to find Hades.
  Ask them to report back.
- **Decoy duty:** Grunts can claim to be Persephone in global chat to
  misdirect Shades.
- **Leadership duty:** Grunts as leaders can protect Persephone from
  hostage selection.

### With Shades Players

- **Minimal engagement after team confirmation.** Don't waste time.
- **Intelligence extraction:** If they're talkative, they might reveal
  Hades's location or whether the Hades-Cerberus exchange is done.
- **Deception opportunities:** Claim to be a Nymph grunt. "Just a grunt,
  nothing interesting here." This deflects interest.
- **Never reveal your role to Shades.** They'll relay to Hades that
  "Demeter is in Room X," which implies Persephone might be reachable
  through you.

### With Unknown Players

- Color exchange first. Standard procedure.
- Prioritize Nymphs teammates over Shades for role exchange probing.
- Be systematic -- track who you've interacted with.

## Demeter vs. Cerberus: Comparative Strategy

| Factor | Demeter | Cerberus |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Partner seeking you? | Persephone is cautious; might hide | Hades is actively seeking |
| Mobility | Free to move rooms | Free to move rooms |
| Post-exchange role | Protect Persephone; share intel | Help position Hades |
| Risk of identity exposure | Moderate (reveals Persephone's network) | Moderate (reveals Shades' plan) |
| Signal strategy | Must be proactive to "wake up" Persephone | Can rely on Hades also searching |

**Key difference:** Demeter must be louder/more proactive than Cerberus
because Persephone is playing defense. Cerberus can rely on Hades also
actively seeking. Demeter's urgency should be a feature, not a bug --
it helps Persephone identify you.

## The Movement Decision

A critical crossroads for Demeter: **should I cross rooms?**

| Scenario | Decision | Reasoning |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Persephone confirmed in your room | Stay | Exchange here |
| All local Nymphs are grunts | Go | Persephone must be in other room |
| Persephone's room unknown | Probe more before deciding | Don't move blindly |
| Persephone in other room, Hades in other room | Risky to go | You'd be bringing Demeter to Hades |
| Persephone in other room, Hades in YOUR room | Go | Other room is safe for both of you |

**The Hades factor:** Before volunteering as hostage, consider whether
going to the other room brings you closer to Hades. If Hades is there,
your arrival doesn't help -- and if you then exchange with Persephone in
Hades's room, she's stuck there. Unless she can then be hostaged out.

Ideally: Demeter goes to Persephone in a room that Hades is NOT in.

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| Can't find Persephone | Critical | Aggressive probing; volunteer as hostage if needed |
| Persephone is hiding from everyone | High | Signal with R.OFFER to all Nymphs; be unmistakably Demeter |
| Moving to Hades's room | Moderate | Gather intel on Hades's location first |
| Revealing identity to Shades | Moderate | Only matters if they can use it to find Persephone |
| Spy impersonating Persephone | Medium | Mutual role exchange reveals truth |
| Time pressure | High | Don't waste interactions on confirmed non-targets |

---

## Decision Heuristics

1. **R.OFFER every Nymphs player you find.** This is your search
   algorithm. Fast, unambiguous, and self-documenting.
2. **If all Nymphs in your room are grunts:** Persephone is elsewhere.
   Volunteer as hostage immediately (Round 2+).
3. **Before moving rooms:** Try to learn where Hades is. Going to Hades's
   room is counterproductive for the team.
4. **If exchange is done:** Shift to intelligence/protection mode. Find
   Hades's location and relay it to Persephone.
5. **Grunts confirmed as non-Persephone are still valuable.** Enlist
   them immediately for intel relay and leadership.
6. **Don't be subtle.** Time pressure makes subtlety a luxury. Demeter's
   job is fast and loud (within Nymphs circles).
